About Us

Firstly let’s remember our childhood days. Born as grandkids of the large family.

We all gather for thatha karya, sometimes even for some festival or function. All grand children under one roof. The time filled with loads and loads of joy and happiness. The time we hated each other. Amma calling you "Alimaiah" which irritated you a lot but brought a smile on my face :-).

Once when we gathered in Batlahalli for some function we started talking to each other. Sat in hot sun and spoke for long time. You tried to take my pics which conveyed something ;-)

One forward message from you made way for our communication to start. Those indirect messages and talks continued... My first visit to chikki alone just to see you! Next day travelling to Reva College for interview. You waiting for me to come back and both went to a hotel together for the first time.

Your Mtech counselling and coming to Bangalore for education. Our formal communication started taking a different track with your first kiss, which made me to express my feelings for you on DEC 9th (year I don’t remember).

You called me 'shilu' which made me feel so happy. We became so close to each other. There were proposals coming for me which was disturbing you so much.

Veenakka marriage, Vani akka marriage, Jaya marriage, Shubha marriage were our meeting spots.

Guru chikkappa talking with us regarding our marriage and asking me to do higher studies. I and anna had come to your college for MCA counselling. Also cannot forget Shilavana and classroom insidents ;-)

You were travelling to Koramangala for your internship at NDS, then got placed there itself.

I must be so thankful to you, for all the support you had given me during my course, projects, and exams and even for getting placed in a company like NDS (which I had never even dreamt of). Your sudden travel to Israel, and our communication was bit difficult as I had no mobile which supports internet. I used to mail or ping you from college, but you were calling me whenever possible. I used to inform atte mava about your status.

Anna health started getting worse day by day, cannot forget your support during that time for me and for my family. You were my strength then. I dono how can I thank you for this!!!! Just before getting my first salary I lost anna :-( I cannot forgive God for doing this to my family :-( (Sorry for adding all these sad things also here).
